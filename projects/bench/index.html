About This Website

The website contains a queue for the bench press in the gym. There are four flat benches at the gym. And people in the queue get moved to the first one that opens.
For a user to join the queue,
  1. type in name and email
  2. press 'Join' button to join the queue
As soon as a flat bench opens up, the user gets an email from bench.press.queue@gmail.com, letting them know that it is their turn and they have 20 minutes to use it. After 20 minutes have passed, the user will get an email notifying them that the time is up.
Users can also work in a pair to use a bench. In that case, the pair gets 40 minutes to use one bench.

Chat

Users can click 'chat' link next to the '?' button to open a webpage for them to chat. Users may use the chat for reasons such as finding a spotter.
